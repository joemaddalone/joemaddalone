Rewatchables

my list of lesser-discussed rewatchable films

I enjoy listening to a variety of podcasts and shows that delve into film discussions, with some favorites like The Rewatchables, Film Sack, Red Letter Media, and Every Frame a Painting. While I find their takes on classic films engaging, I’ve noticed that some of my personal favorite movies rarely get mentioned. With that in mind, I’ve compiled a list of films I consider rewatchable, but that don’t often make it into popular discussions.

Here is my list of lesser-discussed rewatchable films, sorted by their release year:

  • American Pop (1981)
  • The World According to Garp (1982)
  • Deathtrap (1982)
  • Something Wicked This Way Comes (1983)
  • The Keep (1983)
  • Iceman (1984)
  • Teachers (1984)
  • The Stuff (1985)
  • House (1985)
  • At Close Range (1986)
  • River’s Edge (1986)
  • The Hitcher (1986)
  • Weeds (1987)
  • The Serpent and the Rainbow (1988)
  • Clean and Sober (1988)
  • The ‘Burbs (1989)
  • Dead Calm (1989)
  • King of New York (1990)
  • Rush (1991)
  • Hero (1992)
  • Blood In Blood Out (1993)
  • This Boy’s Life (1993)
  • The Last Supper (1995)
  • Murder in the First (1995)
  • Affliction (1997)
  • Run Lola Run(1998)
  • Stir of Echoes (1999)
  • Go (1999)
  • The Prime Gig (2000)
  • The Last Castle (2001)
  • Reign of Fire (2002)
  • One Hour Photo (2002)
  • Equilibrium (2002)
  • Dark Blue (2002)
  • The Salton Sea (2002)
  • Confidence (2003)
  • Open Water (2003)
  • Excision (2008)
  • The Onion Movie (2008)
  • Pandorum (2009)
  • Oculus (2013)
  • Coherence (2013)
  • Circle (2016)

Each film on this list has its own unique charm, and while they might not be widely celebrated, they’re definitely worth a rewatch.
